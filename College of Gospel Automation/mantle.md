Bless thine self with a **Mantle of Holiness**

`!mantle` - Will start your Mantle of Holiness on your active character, using your Bardic Inspiration and Mantle of Holiness counter. If you're in initiative, will add an effect to you.

**Parameters**
`-i` - Ignore requirements, does not use counters.
`-image <image>` - Adds the image to the bottom of the embed
`-thumb <thumb>` - Adds the thumbnail to the embed
